How does the prediction work?

Each account gets its own Random Forest model, trained only on that account's own paid invoices — never on another account's data, and never on a shared demo dataset. It needs at least {{ model_info.invoices_needed or 20 }} paid invoices before there's enough signal to trust over a simple rule.

Below that threshold, predictions use a transparent heuristic based on payment terms (shorter terms and larger amounts are flagged as higher risk) — never presented as machine learning. Every prediction on this page states which one produced it.

Import more invoice history — including which invoices were paid and when — to train (or retrain) the model on real outcomes.
