How does the prediction work?

Smart Pay analyses your customer's payment history to predict whether a new invoice is likely to be paid late. It looks at things like how often the customer has paid on time before, their average delay when they do pay late, whether they have any disputed invoices, and the size of the invoice.

For new customers with no payment history, the system uses the invoice amount and due date to make a conservative estimate. Larger invoices from unknown customers are flagged as higher risk, and invoices that are close to their due date get more attention.

The prediction gets more accurate as you import more payment history data. You can import CSV files with historical invoice data from your accounting system.
