What it does

Three core capabilities, built end-to-end

OCR Invoice Extraction

Upload a PDF or image invoice and Tesseract OCR extracts customer details, amounts, and dates automatically — no manual typing.

ML Payment Prediction

A Random Forest model trained on 2,466 real invoice records predicts which invoices will be paid late, with per-customer reliability scoring.

Smart Dashboard

Risk distribution, payment behaviour, and customer analytics in one view — with automated email reminders that adapt to risk level.

Why late payments matter

Late payments are one of the biggest cash-flow killers for small businesses. Most invoicing tools only tell you an invoice is overdue after it happens.

SmartPay flips that: it learns each customer's payment behaviour and flags the risky invoices up front, so you can chase the right customers before the due date — not weeks after.

  • Per-customer reliability scores built from real payment history
  • Risk-aware email reminders that adapt tone and urgency automatically
  • Explainable predictions — see exactly why an invoice was flagged
