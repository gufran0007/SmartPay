Late payments quietly drain your business

A single overdue invoice can stall payroll, supplier payments and growth. Yet most tools only tell you an invoice is late after the deadline has already passed.

SmartPay flags the risky invoices up front — so you can act while it still makes a difference, instead of writing off the loss weeks later.
